How to view Broomball stats

It's easier than you think.

Navigate to a Broomball team on the Broomball website.

Any old team will do!

Scroll down the page until you get to a table such as the one below.



This is what we call our data table. It holds all of the stats for the team! Why is this important? Because we're going to use this table as the source for our visualization. Keep this page open!

Next we need to open Google Docs

Think of it as an easy way to store your data!

Create a new spreadsheet to store your data in!



But what are we going to fill it with?

Go to back to the table on the Broomball website and copy the entire chart, headers and all and paste it into your Google Spreadsheet!





Sharing is caring!

Right now, your spreadsheet is only visible to yourself. We need to change that! Go to the Share button in the corner and click it! You should be brought to a page with the following settings.



See the link that says change? Click that! You'll be brought to this page.



Make your settings the same as in the picture and click save. The page should now show this. (of course we won't be the owner of your document!)



Get your key!

It unlocks the world of visualizations!

Go to File -> Publish to the web... this will be where you go to get your key!



Start the publishing!



Your key will be in that same popup, just slightly below that top dialog! Your key is the part that is highlighted! Don't lose your key, it's what we will use to create the visualization!



Now you're ready to plot that data!
